About this Tool

The Startup Investor Agent helps synthesize public data and founder‑provided materials to produce a concise, actionable investment memo. It coordinates specialist agents (Data Analyst, Product/Tech Analyst, Risk Analyst) to evaluate market, product quality, defensibility, traction, and risks.

Key Capabilities

  • Structured investment analysis and memo‑ready sections
  • Document‑aware reasoning: images, PDFs, text, and Office files
  • Live streaming responses with clear error messaging

Supported File Types

  • Images: PNG, JPG/JPEG, GIF, WEBP, BMP, TIFF/TIF, SVG
  • Documents: PDF, TXT, CSV
  • Office: Word (DOC/DOCX), PowerPoint (PPT/PPTX), Excel (XLS/XLSX)
  • Size limit: 10 MB per file

Limitations

  • Model understanding of complex Office formats may vary; best results often come from PDFs or extracted text.
  • Large or image‑heavy documents can reduce response quality due to token limits.
  • External data retrieval is limited to what’s provided or publicly accessible.
  • This tool provides analysis, not investment advice. Always perform independent diligence.

Privacy

Uploaded files are sent to the model as part of your session. Avoid sensitive data unless you have reviewed the data handling and storage configuration for your deployment.
